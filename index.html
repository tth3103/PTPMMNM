About Tank Battle

Tank Battle is a casual co-op game where you and your friend need to defeat as many enemies as possible. Set yourself new record and share it with your friends

Instruction: Players

Use A/S/W/D to move and spacebar to shoot as player 1. Use arrow keys to move and right shift to shoot as player 2. Each player starts with 3 lives.

Instruction: Enemies

They have unexpected behaviours, be careful of getting too close to them. Also they come in batch so focus on your surrounding is crucial!

Instruction: Game Rules

15 Enemy spawned in each wave, everytime a wave is cleared a new wave spawned and all players will gain 1 extra lives. Any player that reached 0 lives will be eliminated.
